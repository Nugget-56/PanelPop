To generate your comic panels:

  • Enter a prompt to generate the desired image and wait for it to process. You click the generate button again for a new image if you're not satisfied with the current one
  • After recieving the image you can annotate and edit it as per your needs
  • Select the desired panel by clicking on it and hit confirm to add the image there
  • Customize your panel border colour, width, and gap as per your likeness
  • You can also delete and edit any image from the panel by clicking on it
  • Finally, download and share your awesome comic anywhere!
